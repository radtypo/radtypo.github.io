Manifesto [ + ]

rad.typo is an ongoing exploration of creative documentation – a living archive that captures not just the art, but the entire ecosystem of its creation.

We believe every moment of artistic creation is a complex intersection of:

  • Geographic context
  • Celestial conditions
  • Environmental data
  • Cultural and seasonal rhythms
Our Principle: Let the making be enough. Document the process with the same intentionality as the art itself.

Philosophical Context [ + ]

Inspired by:

  • The radical documentation of performance art
  • DIY music's uncompromising spirit
  • Celtic cyclical understanding of time
  • Ecological interconnectedness

We're not creating a discography. We're mapping a living, breathing creative ecosystem.

Vision: Art as a holistic practice. Documentation as creation. The process is the product.
