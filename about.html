rad.typo: about the site

A music site that feels like a thoughtful conversation, not a data extraction machine. Here's how it works:

The Approach

rad.typo is an experiment in intentional digital making. Technology should connect, not complicate. The site is designed around a simple set of principles:

Content Access

Free Content (GitHub Hosted)

Everything under GitHub storage limits is completely free:

  • MP3 audio files
  • Chord sheets
  • Lyrics
  • Low-resolution artwork

Premium Content (Ko-fi Hosted)

High-quality files available for free:

  • High-resolution artwork
  • Audio stems
  • Extended audio versions
  • Video content

Donations are suggested to fuel future projects, but never required. Access is always free.

rad.typo: faoin suíomh

Suíomh ceoil a bhraitheann mar chomhrá machnamhach, seachas meaisín baint sonraí. Seo mar a oibríonn sé:

An Cur Chuige

Is iniúchadh é rad.typo ar dhéanamh digiteach d'aon turas. Ba chóir don teicneolaíocht ceangal a dhéanamh, gan é a chasta a dhéanamh. Tá an suíomh deartha timpeall ar phrionsabail shimple:

Rochtain ar Ábhar

Ábhar Saor in Aisce (Stóráilte ar GitHub)

Tá gach rud faoi theorainneacha stórála GitHub saor in aisce:

  • Comhaid MP3
  • Bileoga chord
  • Liriceacha
  • Saothair ealaíne íseal-réitigh

Ábhar Préimh (Óstáilte ar Ko-fi)

Comhaid ardchaighdeáin ar fáil saor in aisce:

  • Saothair ealaíne ardréitigh
  • Stemanna fuaime
  • Leaganacha fuaime sínte
  • Ábhar físeáin

Moltar deontas chun tionscadail a thiomáint amach anseo, ach níl aon cheangal air. Tá rochtain i gcónaí saor in aisce.
