"It was fine until that woman opened her mouth."

LWB were a sadly short-lived Scottish band (they split up at about the end of 2002). Everyone in the entire world, ever, seems to have described them as "a cross between [insert any two quasi-punk bands, esp. the Fall, Bow Wow Wow, Television]" but I'd like to pretend that their many idiosyncrasies, in particular the bizarre torrent-of-consciousness lyrics of the singer Sue Tompkins, are at least somewhat original.

Anyway, this page originally had my (and others') transcriptions of the lyrics, but Will Bradley from the band found the page and sent me the real things! For people who like mondegreens, the original transcripts are here.
