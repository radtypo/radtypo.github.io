about this machine

each character of the lyric is classified by phonetic feature. plosives become kick hits, sibilants become hat hits, liquids become toms, vowels pitch the bass, spaces fall on the snare, capitals trigger the clap. ghost notes layer secondary triggers — liquids also tick a soft snare underneath, dark vowels add a sub-thump under the bass — at low velocity.

feel changes the temporal interpretation. straight sits on the grid. lag lays voices on three different clocks at once: kick on the grid, hat in triplet swing, snare pushed slightly early and accented. glitch stays grid-locked but inserts probabilistic 32nd-note flams on hat and snare.

kit swaps the voices. five sampled kits: default (tr-808), paper (linn lm-1, clicky and cold), vinyl (tr-909, punchy and four-floor), felt (linndrum, brushed and soft), wood (roland r-8, warm and live-sounding). starting points worth trying: lag + wood, straight + vinyl, glitch + paper.

drum samples sourced from the tidalcycles ecosystem (sounds-tr808-fischer, tidal-drum-machines) under the project's sample sourcing policy. see notices for per-file attribution.
